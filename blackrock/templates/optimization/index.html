Welcome to the Forest Sampling Design & Optimization Module!

Overview

This simulation aims to teach students the considerations that go into the process of sampling design and thus how to effectively and efficiently sample a forest.

This module is currently being developed by the Center for New Media Teaching and Learning in collaboration with Professor Matt Palmer of the Department of Ecology, Evolution, and Environmental Biology (E3B) at Columbia University.
